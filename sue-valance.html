Sue Valance

A game of profiling

Company University of Plymouth
Job Title Interaction Design Student
Technologies Arduino, MQTT, HTML

A game of profiling. Reengineering two traditional rotary phones that connect only to one another, the installation places two strangers on a phone call, tasked with constructing portraits of their subject using the tools provided. A thought provoking piece that places the surveiller at the hands of the surveilled, hinting at themes of sousveillance and social roles.
