Wishes of the World

An interactive piece commissioned for the Chinese New Year Festival

Company University of Plymouth
Job Title Interaction Design Student
Technologies Arduino, Processing, MQTT, Node Red, HTML, CSS, Javascript

Taking a spin on the traditional wishing lanterns, these interactive installations, commisoned as part of Plymouth's Chinese New Year Festival, illuminate as they are interacted with. Connected as a series across multiple locations, the intensity of the lights correspond to their level of interaction. Relaying their inputs online, users are able to view interactions at this festival in real-time.
