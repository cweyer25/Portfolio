Zephyrus

An Arduino Wind Powered Installation

Company University of Plymouth
Job Title Interaction Design Student
Technologies Arduino, Proccessing, Node Red, MQTT

A self-sustaining, wind powered Arduino system highlighting the untapped green energy source freely available to the University of Plymouth. With the project theme of 'Visualizing the Invisible' we chose to visualize the potential of renweable energy on our campus buildings. The video below documents the building of this project.
